Detailed Description

Organizes test statistics on artificial light curves.

LcBinStats can collate the results from multiple runs and print summary statistics to log files.

The following statistics are supported:

  • C1
  • Period
  • Δm-Δt-based timescales
  • ACF-based timescales
  • peak-finding timescales
